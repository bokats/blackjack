Blackjack

Game Rules

    The objective of the game is to beat the dealer in one of the following ways:
  • Get 21 points on the player's first two cards (blackjack)
  • Reach a final score higher than the dealer without exceeding 21
  • Let the dealer draw additional cards until his or her hand exceeds 21
  • The player can only split cards once
  • The dealer will hit a soft 17
  • The game is played with 6 decks of cards
  • User can only bet $10 per hand and starts with $1000
